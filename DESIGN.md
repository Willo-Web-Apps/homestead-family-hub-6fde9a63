# Design DNA — Hearthstead

## Visual Personality: organic-natural
## Layout: storytelling
## Typography: elegant-serif

### Color Palette
- Primary (Forest Green): oklch(0.36 0.080 140) → #3D5A2E
- Accent (Harvest Amber): oklch(0.60 0.120 55) → #C4773B
- Surface (Parchment Cream): oklch(0.985 0.008 85) → #F7F1E8
- Text (Dark Earth): oklch(0.18 0.025 75) → ~#2A2318

### Font Pairing
- Display: Cormorant Garamond — weights 400, 500, 600, 700 (italic variants) — dramatic editorial serif for headlines; gives the brand gravitas and warmth
- Body: Lora — weights 400, 500, italic 400 — a warm humanist serif for body text; pairs beautifully with Cormorant and reinforces the editorial-farmhouse feel

### Unique Design Choices
1. **Full-bleed hero with multi-layer earthy gradient + grain texture** — three asymmetric radial glows in forest green and harvest amber over parchment cream, plus SVG fractal noise at 3.5% opacity. Feels like golden-hour light streaming through leaves. No photography required.
2. **Typographic sculpture in Origin Story section** — oversized Cormorant "H" letterform sits behind the stat card grid as a decorative anchor, creating a magazine-editorial feel rather than a blog post layout. Asymmetric grid (text left, visual composition right).
3. **Unique organic wave SVG dividers** — each section transition uses a different hand-tuned Bézier curve, mimicking rolling hills and natural contours. No two waves are identical. Reinforces the organic-natural personality throughout.
4. **Pull-quote in forest green with amber left-border accent** — typographic device from editorial print design; gives the story section texture, rhythm, and editorial authority.
5. **Invitation-first CTAs** — "Start My Family's Journey", "Join Our Community". First-person, warm, never salesy. CTA section uses golden-hour gradient to feel like being welcomed into a farmhouse kitchen.

### CEO Design Notes
All 4 major competitors use dated white WordPress blog layouts. Hearthstead is the designed contrast: art-directed editorial magazine meets warm family farm. Serif throughout, generous parchment whitespace, organic shapes. No blues, teals, or purples anywhere.
